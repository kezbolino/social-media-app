Menu & Settings

📍 Saved locations

Your regular pitches. These show as quick-tap buttons when you make a post.

📸 My chicken photos

Add your food photos once — they're saved on this phone, so every post can grab random ones. No internet needed. Tap ✕ to remove a photo.

🍗 Best sellers & sauces

Used to fill food captions (picked at random). Leave empty to skip food captions.

#️⃣ Hashtags

Tapped in on the caption screen — a shuffled, relevant mix each time, plus your pitch location auto-tagged. Tap one to remove it.

💬 My captions

Write your own lines — they join the shuffle with the built-in ones. Tip: type {location} or {day} and the app fills them in. Tap ✕ to remove one.

🔊 Sounds

Little taps, wins and swipe sounds as you use the app. Turn them off if you'd rather keep it quiet.

🔔 Post reminders

Get a nudge to post on the days you're working (set those in the Work calendar).

💾 Backup & restore

Everything above — locations, hashtags, your captions, calendar, queue, post history and saved photos — lives only on this phone. Export it to one file so a new phone or a cleared browser doesn't start you from zero. (Meta credentials below aren't included — reconnect those after restoring.)

🚀 Auto-posting (Meta) — optional

Post straight to Instagram/Facebook without the share sheet. Needs a one-time Meta setup — see docs/META_SETUP.md for the plain-English walkthrough. Everything you paste here stays on this device.
